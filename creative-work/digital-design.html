Digital Design

Digital Pattern making combines traditional garment construction techniques with cutting-edge technology. My pattern making skills include drafting, draping, and adjusting for fit and style, ensuring each design meets both aesthetic and functional standards. With CLO 3D, I take this further by creating realistic, 3D virtual garments that allow for detailed visualization, fit testing, and fabric simulation.
