This scarf is crafted from dyed cotton yarns, naturally dyed using plant-based ingredients, creating a sustainable and Eco-friendly piece. The yarn is sourced from “Organic Textiles org”. The earthy color palette, achieved through herbal dyeing techniques. The soft, breathable cotton provides comfort and durability, while the intricate knit pattern adds depth and texture to the design. Infused with the calming, organic properties of natural dyes, this garment offers a unique connection between fashion and wellness. It’s a reflection of my commitment to creating mindful, sustainable designs that honor both the wearer and the environment.
