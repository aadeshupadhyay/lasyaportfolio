A Guide To Natural Colors

EARTHLY SKIN

Earthly Skin is a scarf crafted from organic cotton yarn and naturally dyed with plant-based ingredients. Its earthy tones are achieved through herbal dyeing techniques, while the intricate knit structure adds texture and depth. Soft, breathable, and sustainable, the piece reflects a commitment to mindful design, celebrating the connection between the body, nature, and responsible craftsmanship.
