Indian Weaves

Kalamkari Calicos – The Indigo Series

Kalamkari Calicos marry hand-drawn, plant-dyed Indian motifs with soft, durable cotton, turning each fabric into wearable art; my designs honor this heritage while giving it a contemporary, formal edge.

Kadhavastram – The Tale Of Divinity

Kadhavastram (stories on a cloth) is a design inspired by Kathakali (plays of stories), a classical dance-drama form originating from Kerala, India, renowned for its elaborate costumes, vibrant makeup, and expressive storytelling.
